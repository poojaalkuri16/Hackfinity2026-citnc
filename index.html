Problem Tracks

04 Industry 5.0–Aligned Problem Tracks

These problem tracks are designed around Industry 5.0 principles, focusing on human-centric innovation, sustainability, and resilient systems. Participants are expected to build practical, scalable solutions aligned with real-world industry challenges using emerging technologies like AI, IoT, and data analytics.

01

Smart Manufacturing & Industry 5.0

Industry Focus

Automotive, aerospace manufacturing, robotics, precision engineering.

Problem Focus

Create AI or IoT solutions for predictive maintenance, factory efficiency, and worker safety.

Expected Output

Manufacturing dashboard, digital twin, or smart factory monitoring system.

02

Human–AI Collaboration

Industry Focus

AI, IT services, smart manufacturing, enterprise automation, healthcare AI.

Problem Focus

Build AI assistants and human-in-the-loop systems that improve productivity and decision-making.

Expected Output

Increased productivity & efficiency, improved decision-making quality, system reliability & safety, skill augmentation, scalable & adaptive project systems, explainable AI dashboard, or collaboration platform.

03

Sustainability & Green Technologies

Industry Focus

Renewable energy, green manufacturing, ESG compliance.

Problem Focus

Develop solutions to monitor and reduce energy use, carbon emissions, and industrial waste.

Expected Output

Carbon tracking dashboard, sustainability analytics platform, or energy monitoring system.

04

Industrial Cybersecurity

Industry Focus

Industrial security, cloud security, enterprise cybersecurity.

Problem Focus

Build systems to detect and prevent cyber threats in industrial networks and IoT devices.

Expected Output

Threat detection model, SOC dashboard, or cybersecurity monitoring platform.

FAQs

Got questions about HackfiniX? We’ve got you covered. Explore everything you need to know—from registration and submissions to rules, rewards, and event details.

What is HackfiniX?

HackfiniX is a 24-hour hackathon designed to foster innovation and creativity by challenging participants to solve real-world problems aligned with Industry 5.0. It brings together students to collaborate, build impactful solutions, and showcase their technical and problem-solving skills.

Participants can explore the detailed problem statements in the Problem Statements section.

Who can participate in HackfiniX?

Open to undergraduate students pursuing BE/BTech, BCA/MCA, BSc (CS), and other relevant degrees.

What is the team size allowed for participation?

Each team must consist of 3 to 4 members. Teams can be formed across different departments and semesters, encouraging diverse perspectives and collaborative problem-solving.

What is the last date for team registration and PPT submission?

The last date for team registration and submission of the PPT (in PDF format) is 22nd April 2026.

What is the selection/shortlisting process?

Teams will be shortlisted based on the evaluation of their submitted presentation (PPT), which must be uploaded in PDF format. The file should be named in the format: team_name.pdf. Submissions will be assessed on the basis of idea clarity, innovation, and relevance to the given problem statements.

Is there any registration fee for HackfiniX?

Shortlisted teams are required to pay a registration fee of ₹100 per participant.

What should the PPT include?

Participants are required to follow the official PPT template provided by the organizers. The template, along with the rulebook and brochure, is available on our website and Unstop under the “Download Attachments” section. Teams are advised to adhere to the given format and guidelines while preparing their submission.

What are the evaluation criteria for shortlisting/final judging?

Projects will be evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Innovation & Creativity
  • Technical Implementation
  • Functionality & Feasibility
  • Impact & Relevance
  • Presentation & Demonstration

The decision of the judging panel will be final and binding.

Will participants receive certificates or rewards?

Yes, HackfiniX offers an exciting prize pool worth ₹2.6 lakh, along with additional rewards and gifts. All participants will also receive certificates of participation.

Where will HackfiniX be conducted?

HackfiniX will be held at Cambridge Institute of Technology North Campus, Bangalore.

Will accommodation and food be provided?

Yes, accommodation and food will be provided for all participants throughout the duration of the hackathon.

Whom should participants contact for queries or support?

For any queries or support, participants can find the relevant contact details at the end of our homepage on the official website, as well as on our Unstop page. Feel free to reach out to us for assistance.
